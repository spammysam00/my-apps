Progressive Overload Rules
↑ Go up in weight
You hit the top of your rep range on both sets with good form. Example: target 6–8 reps, you hit 8 on both sets → increase weight next session.
→ Stay at this weight
You hit the rep range but not the top. Example: hit 7 on set 1, 6 on set 2 → stay at this weight and aim for the top of the range next session.
↓ Consolidate
You didn't hit the bottom of the rep range. Example: target 6–8, only got 5 → stay at this weight and focus on form before going heavier.
Flex Day rule
Always use 50–60% of your main session weight. Stop 3–4 reps short of failure. This is active recovery, not a main session.
